About Kirana Club

Kirana Club is digitising India's unorganised retail sector — a ₹50 lakh crore market that remains almost entirely offline. We build payments infrastructure, B2B ordering platforms, and AI-powered forecasting tools that let kirana owners run smarter businesses.

Since our Series B in 2023, we've grown from 20 to 150 people, launched in 8 cities, and now serve 12 million kiranas monthly. Backed by Sequoia India, Lightspeed, and Tiger Global.
